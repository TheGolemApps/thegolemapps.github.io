Privacy Policy for Beyond Label

Beyond Label is a free app that provides food-related information using data from Open Food Facts (OFF). This application does not require the creation of an account; therefore, we do not collect any personal data for registration or authentication purposes.

Data Source

Data Origin: The food data displayed in Beyond Label is extracted directly from the Open Food Facts database.

License and Conditions: Open Food Facts distributes its data under the Open Database License (ODbL). Please refer to the OFF website for detailed information on the license and any usage requirements, which may include attribution obligations.

Data Collection and Use

Beyond Label simply displays the information sourced from Open Food Facts. We do not incorporate or store any personal data from users.

We may collect non-identifiable analytical data (such as usage statistics and app performance metrics) to improve the service. In addition, we may collect a unique device identifier for remarketing purposes. This information does not allow for the direct identification of users.

Security

We implement industry best practices to secure the data from Open Food Facts and any collected usage statistics. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
