How to Delete Your Personal Data

If you no longer wish to use Smart Weight Tracker and want to delete all your personal data, follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the app and log in using the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to the app menu and select the option to Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions and confirm when prompted.

ATTENTION: Once confirmed, all your personal data will be permanently deleted from our database and cannot be recovered.
